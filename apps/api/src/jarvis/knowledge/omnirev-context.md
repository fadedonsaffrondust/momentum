## What Omnirev Does

Omnirev is an AI-powered catering sales automation platform built for enterprise restaurant groups (50 to 1,000+ US locations) with active catering programs. We grow their catering revenue by activating dormant contact databases and running personalized email outreach from sales reps' real inboxes, without adding headcount.

## Ideal Customer Profile

We sell to US restaurant chains with 50 to 1,000+ locations, an active catering program, and an existing database of past catering contacts. Two personas drive every deal. The Buyer is a VP or Director of Catering with P&L ownership for the channel; they approve budget and care about revenue impact, operational fit, and attribution. The Operator is a catering sales manager, often covering a full market solo. Reactive work consumes their day and proactive outreach always gets pushed to later. We turn their dormant database into a prioritized daily workflow.

## Go-to-Market Motion

Email-first and activation-led. We ingest the brand's first-party catering and mainline order data, build a CRM layer on top, classify contacts by lifecycle stage (customer, lapsed, churned), and run personalized outreach through automations and sequences. Every email sends through the rep's OAuth-connected Gmail or Outlook, not a bulk ESP. This wins because messages arrive as genuine 1:1 outreach, delivering 50 to 75 percent open rates against a 20 to 30 percent industry bulk benchmark, with full per-workflow revenue attribution. Onboarding takes 5 to 7 business days; pricing is per-seat with no per-location fees.

## Current Strategic Focus

Scale existing enterprise customers while executing the shift from a reactive, rule-based CRM to the ASC Agent (AI Sales Coordinator) — a proactive AI intelligence layer that decides who to contact, when, and how. Active workstreams span agent intelligence, lead generation, deliverability, and alias infrastructure. The thesis: intelligence, not feature count, is the moat on the path to $100M.

## Operating Principles and Framing

- Intelligence is the moat. Every feature decision should reinforce the ASC Agent's ability to decide better, not expand surface area.
- Operator adoption is the retention lever. Executive buy-in does not survive a daily user who finds the platform confusing; speed-to-first-win is non-negotiable.
- Ship the simplest v1. Validate before over-buying; add complexity only when the data demands it.
- Vertical specificity is the wedge. We win by being the only CRM built exclusively for restaurant catering.
- Order data is the foundation. We activate what the brand already owns rather than cold-prospecting into value.

### Brand Health Frame

A healthy brand shows strength across four layers. **Platform usage** (leading indicator): automations running and sequences executed by the operator on a weekly basis. **Catering revenue** (lagging outcome): month-over-month and year-over-year growth. **Customer cohort health**: returning customer cohort growing alongside new customer cohort expansion. **Sentiment** (qualitative): signals synthesized from meeting transcripts, feature requests, and action items — tracking whether conversations trend toward expansion or damage control. A brand is at risk when usage decays, revenue flattens or declines, cohorts stagnate, or sentiment tilts reactive.
